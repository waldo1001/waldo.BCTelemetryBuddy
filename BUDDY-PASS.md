# Buddy Pass — Supporting BC Telemetry Buddy

BC Telemetry Buddy is free and open source under the MIT license. You can use it, modify it, and build on it without restriction.

## The Fair Deal

If you use BC Telemetry Buddy as part of work you charge for — whether that's analysing a client's BC environment, building a monitoring service, or delivering telemetry insights as part of a paid engagement — consider sponsoring development. You're using a free tool to earn; a small contribution keeps it maintained and growing.

This isn't a legal requirement. It's a fair deal between professionals.

## What "Buddy Pass" Means

A Buddy Pass is not a license key, a certificate, or anything you need to manage. It's a shorthand for:

> *"You support the development of the tools you depend on. You're covered — thanks."*

At **Supporter** and **Backer** tier you receive a "Powered by BC Telemetry Buddy" badge you can use on your website or marketing materials to show clients you invest in professional tooling.
